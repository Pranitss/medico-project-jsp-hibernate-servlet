Question About Medicines

Q: What are the common side effects of ibuprofen?

A: Common side effects of ibuprofen include stomach pain, heartburn, nausea, and headache.

Q: Can I take paracetamol with antibiotics?

A: Generally, it is safe to take paracetamol with antibiotics, but it's always best to consult with your doctor or pharmacist to ensure there are no interactions.

Q: What should I do if I miss a dose of my medication?

A: If you miss a dose of your medication, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule. Do not double the dose to catch up.

Q: How should I store my prescription medications?

A: Prescription medications should be stored according to the instructions provided on the label or by your healthcare provider. Typically, medications should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Some medications may require refrigeration.
